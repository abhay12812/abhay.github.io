Introduction

SAMAST PATIDAR AAROGYA TRUST is a “NOT FOR PROFIT” Organization. The Trust has setup a 900 bedded state-of-the-art Multi Super Specialty Hospital and Research Centre in Surat, Gujarat. The hospital serves patients not only from Gujarat but across India and internationally.

Our Achievements

On April 17, 2017, Kiran Hospital was inaugurated by the Prime Minister of India, Shri Narendra Modi. Over 7 years, more than 27 Lac patients have been treated.

400 crore worth of advanced equipment has been used for diagnosis in 45 departments, supported by 105 super-specialists and 300 specialist doctors.

Community Service

Out of every 10 patients, 4 free operations are performed under the Ayushman Bharat Yojana, helping lower-income families.

Last 7 years, Kiran Hospital has provided OPD benefits worth 504 crores, and free treatments worth 588 crores under Ayushman Bharat Yojana.

Affordable Healthcare

Consultation charges are much lower than other hospitals:

  • ✅ Super-Specialty Doctor Fee: ₹250 (Compared to ₹1500 in city hospitals)
  • ✅ Specialist Doctor Fee: ₹150 (Compared to ₹500 - ₹800 in city hospitals)
  • ✅ Monthly OPD savings for patients: ₹6 crore

Comparative Benefits

Kiran Hospital provides over 8000 free treatments per month under Ayushman Bharat Yojana.

Our pathology, radiology, and critical services (MRI, CT Scan, Dialysis, Chemo, etc.) are 25-40% cheaper than market rates.
